A heaping handful of excited energy, a pinch of disorganization, two and half teaspoons of minimalism to balance, and most importantly, a few healthy pinches of wishes, whimsy, and wonder. Stir for a while, and you get a junior at the University of Southern California named Gavin. Majoring in Computer Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Cinematic Arts emphasizing in Game Design, I love learning, building, and following the philosophy of "if it's fun and interesting, it's worth understanding and doing."
